Pose
A team-based university project designed to improve desk health. Our final concept measures back posture using pressure sensors in a seat-pad. This measurement is sent to a desk companion via Bluetooth which mimics the user’s posture, providing a gentle nudge. A hand-held carpet cleaner for single parents
The brief was to design an electronic device for single parents. It was found that wet-messes are a constant battle for this user group who are always short on time. This product can be left on wet-messes and stains such that when the parent returns, it is clean. Skills: conducting interviews with users, product design, user-based design, cardboard prototyping, SOLIDWORKS.

Soft Robotics To Help With Yoga Teacher Training
This project is a conceptual prototype for a soft robotics device using FlowIO to help with yoga aligment. The product is a belt with sensors to detect imbalance in the hips. The FlowIO inflates to apply pressure on the side that needs lowering.​ Special thanks to my Professor Thrishantha Nanayakkara and the creator of FlowIO Ali Shtarbanov.
